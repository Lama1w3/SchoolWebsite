Institution Overview

Nestled in the serene Satluj Valley township of Jhakri, in Shimla district of Himachal Pradesh, Government Utkrisht Senior Secondary School, Jhakri stands as a flagship institution under the Government of Himachal Pradesh’s visionary “Utkrisht Vidyalaya” initiative.

More than just a secondary school, it represents a transformative educational model — designed to elevate government schooling to a standard comparable with premier national institutions, while preserving accessibility, inclusivity, and regional identity.

Our Journey of Transformation

Originally established to serve the children of Jhakri and neighboring villages — including families associated with the Nathpa Jhakri Hydro Power Project — the school steadily emerged as a center of academic promise in upper Shimla.

Recognizing its academic growth, infrastructure readiness, and regional significance, the Government of Himachal Pradesh conferred upon it the prestigious “Utkrisht” status.

  • Strategic infrastructure modernization
  • Strengthened faculty deployment
  • ICT-integrated smart classrooms
  • Advanced laboratory facilities

Vision

To nurture intellectually capable, ethically grounded, and technologically empowered young citizens who contribute meaningfully to both the Himalayan region and the nation at large.

Mission

  • Deliver equitable, high-quality secondary education.
  • Integrate skill-based learning aligned with emerging industries.
  • Promote environmental stewardship in an eco-sensitive Himalayan zone.
  • Empower girls through academic excellence and leadership opportunities.
  • Build disciplined, culturally rooted, socially responsible youth.

Vocational Innovation

Automobile Technology Lab

Equipped with engine cut-sections, gearbox models, and diagnostic tools, this lab bridges classroom learning with real-world mechanical skills — preparing students for technical apprenticeships and entrepreneurship.

Tourism & Hospitality Lab

Reflecting Himachal Pradesh’s tourism-driven economy, this lab trains students in front office management, guest services, eco-tourism practices, and hospitality fundamentals.

Technology-Enabled Learning

The institution integrates modern educational technologies including:
