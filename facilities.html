Modern infrastructure built to inspire learning, discipline and excellence.

ICT Laboratories

Our school has two fully equipped ICT labs with modern computer systems, internet connectivity and smart digital tools to enhance technical knowledge.

Biology Laboratory

Well-maintained biology lab with microscopes, models and practical equipment for hands-on scientific learning.

Chemistry Laboratory

Equipped with advanced apparatus and safety measures for safe and effective experimentation.

Physics Laboratory

Modern instruments that help students understand core scientific principles through real experiments.

Automobile Lab

Skill-based vocational training facility providing practical knowledge of vehicle systems and mechanics.

Mid Day Meal

Providing nutritious and balanced meals to support students’ health, well-being, and active learning throughout the school day.
