About Our Institution

Govt. Utkrisht Sr. Sec. School, Jhakri, is a premier educational institution committed to academic excellence and holistic development. Established with a vision to empower young minds, the school provides quality education under CBSE curriculum.

Located in the scenic region of Shimla district, our institution fosters innovation, discipline, and leadership among students through modern teaching methods and co-curricular activities.
